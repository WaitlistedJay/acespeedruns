How to Read These Notes

These notes were originally written up by indykenobi for his own use. The recent re-release of Ace Attorney has changed the court record UI. This update was written by Splicerchild, with the help of SuperbKingz24.
Here’s how to decode the short hand:

Presenting

  • In Court:
    S# EX.Y (…) - this means on statement #, Present the Evidence (E) on page X in slot Y, where the slots are numbered 1-10 from left to right. The stuff in parentheses is shorthand for the cursor movements; for example, R.R means to go right, then right. U means to scroll one page to the right (press Up) (D means down -> page to the left).
    Present EX.Y (…) - this means the evidence menu will pop up automatically; present the evidence as described above.
  • In Investigations:
    Present EX.Y (…) - this means enter the Present Menu, and present the evidence/profile as described above. Sometimes the Present menu will pop up automatically.

Pressing

  • S# P means Press the # statement in the testimony.
  • SA P means Press all statements in the testimony
  • SN P means Press the new statement (no movement necessary).
  • SN+X P means Press the statement that is X statements after the new one

Miscellaneous

  • X/Y means to choose the Xth option out of Y choices; so for example, Opt 2/3 is to choose the second of three choices. This applies to Talk, Opt, and Move.
